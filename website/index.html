Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Narayani Waterpark, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you how we look after your personal data when you visit our website or book tickets.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your Name, Phone Number, and Email Address solely for the purpose of booking confirmation and communication regarding your visit.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to generate your e-ticket, process payments via PayU, and provide updates via WhatsApp or SMS.

3. Data Security

Your payment details are handled securely by PayU. We do not store sensitive credit card or banking information on our servers.

Terms & Conditions

By purchasing a ticket or entering Narayani Waterpark, you agree to the following terms:

1. Entry Rules

  • Standard swimwear is mandatory for all water attractions.
  • Outside food and beverages are not allowed inside the park.
  • Kids under 12 must be accompanied by an adult.

2. Safety & Liability

Follow all lifeguard instructions and ride guidelines. The park management is not responsible for injuries resulting from negligence or failure to follow safety rules.

3. Rights of Admission

Management reserves the right of admission and may ask guests to leave if rules are violated.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation

Tickets booked online are non-cancellable once the payment is processed. However, you may request a date change at least 24 hours before your visit date, subject to availability.

2. Refunds

Refunds are only processed in case of full-day park closure due to exceptional technical reasons or management decisions. No refunds will be given due to weather conditions like rain (the park stays open during rain).

3. No-Show

If you fail to visit the park on your selected date, no refund or rescheduling will be provided.
